Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare

On November 2, 1984, the nightmare began in the town of Springwood, U.S.A. when the innocence of Elm Street was violated for eternity. The Springwood PTA ignored their children's claims that an evil man named Freddy Krueger was the Dream Monster responsible for the mysterious and often violent deaths of their friends. The PTA knew the Krueger name, and they knew he was a killer once. They also knew that Freddy Krueger was killed in a vigilante action that many of them participated in; how could he possibly be responsible for the inexplicable terror plaguing Springwood's youth? With Freddy's Dead: The Final Nightmare, they've saved the best for last. Evil dreamstalker Freddy Krueger is dispatched forever in this thrilling, final installment of the phenomenally successful Nightmare series.
