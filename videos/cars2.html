2022 Lexus IS 350 F Sport Review - Walk
Around and Test Drive

Lexus report that their F Sport series takes inspiration from their Lexus LFA Supercar, which you can tell from its first look. It is stylish, sporty, and muscular.The philosophy of the IS 350 F Sport is to take sports styling and performance to new heights with a sports-focused cockpit and fine-tuned handling. From the first look, the Lexus IS 350 F Sport is a head-turner thanks to its distinctive spindle grille, mesh inserts, and chrome detailing. In terms of what's under the hood, which will also likely turn heads, you will find a 311 hp naturally aspirated V6 and an automatic transmission. It is super powerful and smooth thanks to its aerodynamic fins and front and rear suspension, which have been set up and finely tuned to enhance handling and ride quality.
