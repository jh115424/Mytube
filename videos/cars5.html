Sweet Ambient Lights! Mercedes-Benz
C-Class Night Review (Exterior, Interior)

The 2022 Mercedes-Benz C-Class is the new fifth iteration of the brand's namesake model. With a complete ground-up redesign, the W206 C-Class's late arrival in spring due to global shortages left many wondering if the replacement would make this year at all. Thankfully, Mercedes-Benz is leaving many of the manufacturing woes in the past as inventory begins to fill dealer lots. The fifth-generation C-Class is an elegant luxury car that arrives in sedan flavor only; convertible and coupe variants will follow in 2023. The all-new C-Class is sure to a attract many first-time Mercedes-Benz buyers, even at its decidedly premium price tag.
