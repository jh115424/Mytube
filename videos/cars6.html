New Range Rover Sport | Power,
redefined Dramatic meets dynamic.

The Range Rover Evoque started as a concept vehicle introduced to the world as the Land Rover LRX. The concept made its first public appearance at the North American International Auto Show in 2008, with its first production version in 2011. The car swooned the masses with its jaw-dropping coupe-like exterior design and off-road capabilities. The top-tier element of luxury possessed by the subcompact SUV made this brute the ultimate package. The second generation of the Evoque began its run in 2018 and sported a host of exterior and performance upgrades, with its already appealing personality becoming more robust. The SUV had to capitalize on its strong image in the automotive sector and managed to pull off this incredible feat with relative ease.
